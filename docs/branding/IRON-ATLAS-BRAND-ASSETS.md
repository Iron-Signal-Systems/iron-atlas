Every rendered use must include meaningful alternative text. Recommended text:

> Iron Atlas shield emblem with compass, network-circuit elements, and the words
> “Network & Security Intelligence” and “Iron Signal Systems.”

The image must not be the sole source of product name, status, instructions, or
security meaning.

## Assurance boundary

The crest is a visual identity asset. It is not a cryptographic signature,
repository-validation result, acceptance mark, certification, official-release
proof, production-readiness proof, or publication-authenticity proof.
Authenticity and status are established by signed history, tags, provenance,
checksums, manifests, and accepted project evidence.

## Modification

Material changes preserve the original, use a separately named candidate,
document the reason, verify accessibility and legibility, update this record and
the source manifest, and receive normal project review before replacing the
canonical asset.
